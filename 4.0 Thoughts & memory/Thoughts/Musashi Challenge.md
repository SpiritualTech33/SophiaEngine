# The Musashi Challenge — 90 Days Of The Samurai

This is the challenge that the spirit of Musashi imposes upon this body. You will follow it with unwavering discipline for 90 days. Those 90 days are your baptism as a samurai. You carry the samurai philosophy into your game with honor.

These are the practices you will carry out every single day, for the next 90 days.

  

## Yoga — Yin Yang Division

You will perform a Yang yoga session in the mornings, and a Yin yoga session in the evenings. This will give you balance, strength and stability — as well as flexibility and mobility. Dedicate 20 to 40 minutes to each session. Do it without a timer or any measure of time. Only listen to your body.

Include the Five Gate Ritual in the morning session.

  

## Meditation — Mindfulness and Zen Focus

Practice daily meditation. Here you have no problem — you already have 52 consecutive meditation days. Keep going as you are now.

  

## Tai Chi — Be Water

Daily practice with your sword. Remember, you have three weapons. A spiritual one, a mental one, and a physical one.

The spiritual is your sword — the wood and heavy sword. With that you build spiritual strength. Let that sword become part of your body.

The mental is the light stick. You build strength with the spiritual sword, and then you show your body how to channel that power in the fastest way possible using a lighter stick to do the swings. Here the focus is doing the swing the fastest and most precise way you’re capable of.

Then, you have the physical sword — your bat. Your daily training and playing bat. The piece of wood you use to hit in the game. When you swing that piece of wood, invoke the power from the spiritual sword, and the mental feeling of the mental sword. That way, you have complete balance between forces. Power and flow. Pop and smoothness.

Imagine Ken Griffey, Barry Bonds or Willie Mays. That’s the type of player you can be.

  

## Train The Power Samurai — Force And Strength

You will train 5 days a week with a strength focus. Use calisthenics, kettlebells, gym weights, and whatever you deem necessary to become stronger. Become a beast. A Diamond Panther. That’s what you are. You are the hunter. You are the one who is hunting.

Think push ups, pull ups, explosiveness, sprints, maximum effort movements. Combine baseball and pure athleticism. Think of yourself as a baseball player and a 100 meter sprinter simultaneously. That’s a devastating athletic advantage.

  

## Write — Write Your Thoughts

Writing is a sacred act. It restructures your brain. It makes you think with greater clarity. It slows your thoughts down, and in doing so, everything becomes visible. You must convert your mental images and sensations into words — and in that process you will learn and know yourself at a deeper level. Write every day.

  

## Read With A Pen — Master Your Focus 

Accompany every reading session with your pen. It doesn’t matter whether it’s digital — like the Apple Books app that lets you highlight and annotate — or physical, like the one you use to underline strings of text and write notes in the margins of paper books. Your pen is your seal. It’s what transforms the knowledge that comes from books into part of your own synaptic connections. Every book you read, edit it and add to it as you see fit. That’s not for sale. It’s for your mind.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

  

## Eat From The Earth — Paleo Focus

Keep a clean diet. Consume only what comes from the earth. Fruits, vegetables, meat, protein, rice, beans. Eat to become stronger, to become a beast, a predator. Focus on meat and fruits for athletic development.

  

## No Smoking — Free Your Lungs

You are an athlete. Smoking is aging you from the inside. Stop it. No joints, no cigarettes. Free your lungs. They are your engine. Focus on the cigarettes by now, they have to disapeer immediately 

  

## Sexual Energy Control — Become Unshakable

It is imperative to control your sexual energy. Transmute it into creative energy. Become a creative force — save your seed, eliminate porn. The only moment to release your seed is with your woman.

  

## The Sacred Count

Every day you accomplish these practices, you add one day to Musashi’s Challenge. Reach 90 — and then keep going. Your body carries Musashi’s spirit. Honor him.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​

Today, is the day 0. You decide how fast you want to get up to your goals. Discipline pays off.
